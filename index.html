Welcome to my website dedicated to the exquisite art of Palestinian embroidery, also known as tatreez. Delve into the rich cultural heritage of Palestine through intricate designs, motifs, and stitches that have been passed down through generations.

Palestinian Thoub Embroidery

The thoub, a traditional Palestinian dress, serves as a canvas for these exquisite embroideries, showcasing the artistry and craftsmanship passed down through generations.

Palestinian thoub embroidery encompasses a rich tapestry of cultural heritage, with each city boasting its own unique style and designs.

From the vibrant motifs of Ramallah to the intricate stitches of Bethlehem, Palestinian embroidery reflects the identity and traditions of its people.
